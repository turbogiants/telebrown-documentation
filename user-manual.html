  1. INTRODUCTION
  1. DESCRIPTION

Telebrown is a mobile messaging application that offers secure messaging and ensures privacy for your communication needs. We use end-to-end encryption to protect the messages you send from others. Not even we can see your messages.

  1. SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

System Requirement

Android Version

At least Android 6.0 - Marshmallow

Processor

Any ARM64 CPU Architecture

Memory

1GB

Storage

Minimum Free 1GB Storage

  1. INSTALLATION

 Steps:

  1. Download the APK from the Google Drive link.
  2. Install the APK into your compatible device.
  3. Launch the Application!

  1. STARTUP

        The Telebrown logo will greet you when you launch the application.

Telebrown Splash Screen

Onboarding

Before you get started, the app needs permission to access your phone’s storage.

                

Registration

After granting permission to the app, you will be given a one time unique ID which you use to register into the app.

        

        

Key Storage

After that you need to choose whether to save your encrypted passphrase in your device or in an external storage.

        

Passphrase

Next, you will be asked to set up a passcode. This passcode will be used to unlock the application.

        

Pin Login

Finally, you will need to enter your passcode again in order to login to the application.

After logging in, you can now enjoy your privacy as you chat with friends and family.

QR Code

After the initial setup, you will be given a QR Code that you can use to connect with other users.

        

Settings

Before you can chat with your friends, you first need to add them to your contact list.

A.1 ADDING CONTACTS (By QR)

You can add contacts by scanning your friends QR Code.

A.2 ADDING CONTACTS (By Unique ID)

You can also add your friends using their unique ID. You would need to enter their exact ID and then you can add them to your contact list.

B.1 CONTACT VERIFICATION

After adding them to your contact list, you need to verify the safety number that you share with them. Once you see that your numbers match, you can now have a conversation with your contact.
